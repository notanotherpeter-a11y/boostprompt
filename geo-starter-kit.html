What is GEO and why do I need it?

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is optimizing your content so AI search engines like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, and Perplexity cite your business when answering user questions. With traditional search volume dropping 25% in 2026 (Gartner), GEO is essential for maintaining online visibility.

How is this different from regular SEO?

Traditional SEO aims to rank in Google's 10 blue links. GEO aims to get cited in AI-generated answers (usually 2-7 sources). AI engines evaluate content differently—they favor clear answers, original data, and strong entity authority over traditional ranking factors.

Do I need technical skills to implement this?

What's llms.txt and why do I need it?

llms.txt is the new standard file (like robots.txt) that guides AI systems on how to interpret your site. It's emerging as a best practice in 2026. Early adopters gain a competitive advantage before it becomes widely known.
